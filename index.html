Why it is needed

Manufacturing sourcing is slow because the work is scattered.

Teams usually move between drawings, emails, supplier directories, spreadsheets, shipping estimates, and privacy concerns before they can even decide who is worth contacting.

01

Missing specs

Suppliers ask for material, finish, tolerance, MOQ, dimensions, and shipping details again and again.

02

Manual discovery

Supplier search evidence is spread across browser tabs and B2B websites.

03

Hidden landed cost

Unit price is not enough. Freight, duty, timeline, and risk change the real decision.

04

Privacy risk

Buyer name and shipping identity should not leak during early supplier discovery.
